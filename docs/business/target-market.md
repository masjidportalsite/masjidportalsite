# Target Market Analysis: MasjidPortalSite

## 1. Segmentation
The market is divided into three main tiers based on organizational size and complexity:

### Tier 1: State/District Mosques (Masjid Negeri/Daerah)
- **Scale:** Large (1,000+ congregants)
- **Needs:** Complex financial reporting, staff management, large-scale event handling, official compliance.
- **Decision Makers:** State Islamic Departments (JAIN) and appointed high-level committees.

### Tier 2: Local Community Mosques (Masjid Kariah)
- **Scale:** Medium (200 - 1,000 congregants)
- **Needs:** Donation tracking, funeral services (khairat), facility booking, community announcements.
- **Decision Makers:** Elected Mosque Committee (JKP).
- **Sweet Spot:** This is our primary target for initial adoption.

### Tier 3: Surau & Small Prayer Halls
- **Scale:** Small (50 - 200 congregants)
- **Needs:** Simple donation management, WhatsApp integration for announcements.
- **Decision Makers:** Local resident committees.

### Tier 4: Islamic NGOs & Educational Centers (Tahfiz/Madrasah)
- **Scale:** Varies
- **Needs:** Student sponsorship management, project-based fundraising.

## 3. Decision-Making Process
1. **Initiation:** A younger committee member (often the "Setiausaha" or "Biro Belia") identifies the need for modernization.
2. **Evaluation:** The committee reviews features, focusing on **Trust** and **Ease of Use**.
3. **Approval:** The "Nazir" (Head) and "Bendahari" (Treasurer) approve based on cost-benefit and transparency.
4. **Implementation:** Data migration from Excel/Paper and training for staff.

## 4. Key Value Propositions
- **For Admin:** "Spend 50% less time on paperwork."
- **For Treasurer:** "Automated, audit-ready financial reports."
- **For Congregation:** "Stay connected with your mosque via mobile."
- **For Donors:** "Transparent tracking of your Sadaqah/Zakat."
