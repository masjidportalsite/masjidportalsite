# Growth Strategy: MasjidPortalSite

## 1. Core Philosophy: Trust-First Growth
In the mosque community, trust is the primary currency. Our growth strategy follows the B.L.A.S.T priority: **TRUST > SECURITY > COMMUNITY VALUE > AUTOMATION > SPEED.**

## 2. Product-Led Growth (PLG) Strategy
We will offer a **Free Tier (Community Edition)** to lower the barrier to entry.
- **Hook:** Beautiful, free-to-use prayer times and announcement display for TVs and mobile.
- **Retention:** Household management and event registration.
- **Conversion:** Premium features like advanced accounting, funeral fund (Khairat) management, and automated financial reporting.

## 3. Phase-Based Roadmap

### Phase 1: Beta & Evangelism (Month 1-3)
- **Target:** 10 "Champion" mosques in diverse urban/rural areas.
- **Action:** Direct onboarding by the team, gathering feedback, and polishing the UI.
- **Goal:** Case studies and testimonials.

### Phase 2: Regional Expansion (Month 4-9)
- **Target:** 100 mosques across Malaysia.
- **Action:** Self-service onboarding portal, online training webinars.
- **Partnerships:** Reach out to JAIN/JAKIM to demonstrate compliance and value.

### Phase 3: Marketplace & Integration (Month 10+)
- **Target:** 500+ mosques.
- **Action:** Open API for local Muslim-owned businesses to offer services (Halal catering, funeral services, Islamic bookstores) through the portal.

## 4. Referral Program: "Bina Jemaah"
We will implement a referral program where mosques that successfully onboard another mosque receive:
- Recognition on the platform as a "Platinum Community Pillar."
- Extended premium features for free.
- Priority support.

## 5. Partnership Strategy
- **Payment Gateways:** Partner with ToyyibPay/Billplz for discounted transaction rates for MasjidPortalSite users.
- **Islamic NGOs:** Partner with bodies like Aman Palestine or MERCY Malaysia to integrate their campaigns directly into mosque portals.
- **State Bodies:** Seek "Approved Vendor" status from JAIS, JAWI, and other state departments.

## 6. Community Engagement
- **Townhalls:** Quarterly online sessions with mosque committees to discuss the product roadmap.
- **User Groups:** WhatsApp/Telegram communities for Mosque Administrators to share best practices.

## 7. PLG Viral Loops
We will leverage the natural community interaction within the mosque ecosystem to drive organic growth:

- **Loop 1: The "Digital TV" Effect:** Our high-fidelity "Emerald" TV display system (SacredCore) acts as a physical billboard. Visiting committees from other mosques often benchmark their own mosque's digital presence against neighbors.
- **Loop 2: Professional Digital Receipts:** Donors receive branded, professional receipts via WhatsApp/Email. The "Powered by MasjidPortalSite" branding on these high-utility documents increases brand awareness among the most active community members (donors).
- **Loop 3: Household Management:** By requiring the "Ketua Keluarga" (Head of Household) to manage their family members on the portal for event registration, we drive multi-user adoption within a single family unit.
