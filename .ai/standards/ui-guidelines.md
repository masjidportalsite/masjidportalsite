# AI Engineering Standard: UI/UX Guidelines

## "Digital Sanctuary" Aesthetic
- **Visuals**: Emerald (`#003527`) and Gold (`#fed65b`) are primary tones. High contrast, rounded terminals.
- **Patterns**: Subtle Islamic geometry in backgrounds. Use backdrop blur (`backdrop-blur-md`) for overlays.
- **Roundedness**: Cards `12px` min; Buttons pill-shaped (`99px`).

## Component Standards
- **shadcn/ui**: Use shadcn primitives for consistency and accessibility.
- **Responsive**: Mobile-first grid systems. Desktop layouts should feel spacious.
- **Interaction**: Visible focus states; active scale reduction (`active:scale-95`).

## Touch-First Targets
- **Size**: Minimum touch target of `44x44px`.
- **Spacing**: Generous padding for thumb-reach navigation on mobile.
- **Persistence**: Long-lived sessions (7 days) for operational continuity.
