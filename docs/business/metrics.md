# Business Metrics & Success Dashboard

## 1. Primary KPIs (The North Star)
- **Total Onboarded Organizations:** Count of mosques, suraus, and NGOs with an active portal.
- **Gross Managed Volume (GMV):** Total value of donations processed through the platform.
- **Monthly Active Admins (MAA):** Number of committee members logging in at least 3 times a week.
- **Congregation MAU:** Number of unique members accessing their mosque portal monthly.

## 2. Growth Metrics
- **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost):** Marketing/Sales spend divided by mosques onboarded.
- **Referral Rate:** % of new mosques coming from the "Bina Jemaah" referral program.
- **Time to Value (TTV):** Average days from sign-up to first donation received or first announcement published.

## 3. Product & Engagement Metrics
- **Feature Adoption Rate:** Which modules are used most (Donations vs. Announcements vs. Household).
- **Sticky Factor:** (Daily Active Admins / Monthly Active Admins) x 100.
- **Support Ticket Volume:** Number of issues raised per 100 onboarded mosques.

## 4. Financial Sustainability
- **Platform Fee Revenue:** (If applicable) Revenue from transaction fees.
- **SaaS Subscription Revenue:** (For premium tiers).
- **Burn Rate:** Monthly operational costs (Vercel, InsForge, APIs).

## 5. Tracking Framework
- **Analytics Tool:** Vercel Analytics for frontend, Custom database queries for business logic.
- **Reporting Frequency:**
  - **Weekly:** Growth and stability update.
  - **Monthly:** Full executive business report.
  - **Quarterly:** Strategic review and roadmap adjustment.

## 6. Initial Targets (6 Months)
- **Onboarded Mosques:** 50
- **Total Donations Processed:** RM 250,000
- **Average MAA per Mosque:** 5
- **Referral Rate Target:** 20%
